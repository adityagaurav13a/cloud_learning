Understanding Civil Cases

Civil cases involve disputes between individuals or organizations. They cover matters such as property rights, contracts, consumer protection, and family disputes. At Judicial Solutions, we provide expert guidance and representation to ensure justice is served with integrity.

Types of Civil Cases

🏠 Property Disputes

Issues like illegal possession, inheritance conflicts, and boundary disputes.

📑 Contract Disputes

Cases of breach of contract, service disagreements, and financial claims.

⚖️ Consumer Protection

Fraudulent services, defective products, and unfair practices.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Family Matters

Divorce, child custody, maintenance, and alimony disputes.
